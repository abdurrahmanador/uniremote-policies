6. Permissions Explained

The App requires these Android permissions (and why):

6.1 Network Permissions

  • INTERNET: Connect to your TV
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Check WiFi connection
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: Scan for TVs on network
  • CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE: Discover devices
  • NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES: Find TVs nearby (Android 13+)

6.2 Service Permissions

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Keep connection alive in background
  • WAKE_LOCK: Prevent disconnection during use
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: Maintain stable connection

6.3 Optional Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Voice control (future feature, not currently used)
